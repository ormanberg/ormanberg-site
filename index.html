About Orman & Berg Capital Group

Orman & Berg Capital Group is a private real estate investment firm dedicated to acquiring and restoring distressed residential and commercial properties throughout the United States. We transform underutilized spaces into thriving, income-generating assets — while building strong, lasting value for our partners.

Our Mission

As part of our commitment to responsible investing, we work to provide affordable housing solutions for families in need. We believe revitalizing properties should not only generate returns, but also strengthen the communities we invest in.

That’s why we also support local nonprofits in the neighborhoods where we operate — helping fund programs that uplift residents and foster long-term stability.

Our Core Principles

  • Value Creation – We see opportunity where others see risk.
  • Community Focused – Affordable housing is central to our restoration efforts.
  • Long-Term Strategy – Our investments are built for lasting impact and growth.
